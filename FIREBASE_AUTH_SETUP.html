Fix the "auth/configuration-not-found" error

‚ùå Current Error

You're seeing: Firebase: Error (auth/configuration-not-found)

This means Google Sign-In is not enabled in your Firebase project.

1

Open Firebase Console

Click the button below to go directly to your Firebase Authentication settings:

üöÄ Open Firebase Console
2

Navigate to Sign-in Methods

In the Firebase Console:

  • Click on "Authentication" in the left sidebar
  • Click on the "Sign-in method" tab at the top
üì∏ You'll see a list of authentication providers
3

Enable Google Provider

Find and enable Google authentication:

  • Find "Google" in the list of providers
  • Click on it to open the settings
  • Toggle the "Enable" switch to ON
  • Select your email as the "Project support email"
  • Click "Save"
4

Verify Authorized Domains

Make sure localhost is authorized:

  • Go to the "Settings" tab in Authentication
  • Scroll to "Authorized domains"
  • Verify that localhost is in the list (it should be by default)
5

Test Sign-In

After enabling Google Sign-In:

  • Refresh your DebugMate app
  • Click the "Sign In with Google" button
  • You should see the Google Sign-In popup!
